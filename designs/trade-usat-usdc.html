USAT / USDC — frequently asked questions FAQ schema

Common questions about swapping between USAT and USDC — including key differences between these two US-regulated dollar stablecoins.

What is the difference between USAT and USDC? comparison query
Both USAT and USDC are USD-pegged stablecoins with a US regulatory focus. USAT is Tether's US-specific stablecoin — a newer token designed specifically to meet US regulatory requirements, backed by Tether's infrastructure. USDC is issued by Circle, a US-registered money services business, with monthly audited reserve reports published publicly. Both are pegged to $1 USD. On Ledn, you can hold either or both and swap between them with zero fees.
Is USDC or USAT better? comparison intent
It depends on your use case. USDC has a longer track record (launched 2018), is widely integrated across DeFi, CeFi, and payment rails, and publishes monthly third-party audited reserve reports. USAT is newer, also US-regulated, and backed by Tether's infrastructure — making it relevant for users or institutions who prefer the Tether ecosystem with US regulatory compliance. On Ledn, you can hold both simultaneously and swap between them instantly at zero cost. You don't have to choose.
Can I earn yield on USDC on Ledn? yield intent
Yes. USDC is available in the Ledn Growth Account to earn yield. After swapping USAT to USDC on Ledn, you can transfer your USDC to the Growth Account to start earning. The full flow happens on-platform — no external wallets or transfers required. Check the Ledn platform for current Growth Account rates.
Can I borrow in USDC on Ledn? loan intent
Yes. Ledn's Bitcoin-backed loans can be disbursed in USDC — as well as USDT or USAT. Deposit Bitcoin as collateral, select USDC as your loan currency, and receive USDC directly to your Ledn Transaction Account. No credit check required. You can then swap the borrowed USDC to USAT at zero cost if preferred.
