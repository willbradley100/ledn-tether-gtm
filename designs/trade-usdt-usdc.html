Questions answered directly from Google's "People Also Ask" for USDT/USDC queries.

Is it free to swap USDT to USDC? PAA #1
On Ledn, yes — swapping USDT to USDC (or USDC to USDT) carries zero trading fees. This is a meaningful differentiator: most centralized exchanges and DEX aggregators charge 0.1%–0.25% for stablecoin conversions. On Ledn, the rate you see when you confirm is the exact rate you receive, with no fees, no spread markup, and no hidden charges. Simply deposit USDT or USDC into your Transaction Account and navigate to Trade to get started.
What's the difference between USDT and USDC? PAA #2
Both USDT (Tether) and USDC (USD Coin) are stablecoins pegged to the US Dollar — meaning 1 USDT ≈ $1.00 and 1 USDC ≈ $1.00. The main differences are in issuer and transparency. USDC is issued by Circle, a US-regulated company, and backed by audited reserves of US dollars and short-term Treasury bonds. USDT is issued by Tether Ltd. and has the largest stablecoin market cap globally — giving it the deepest liquidity across most exchanges and trading pairs. Ledn supports both, as well as USAT, so you can hold whichever suits your needs.
Is USDT or USDC safer? PAA #3
Both are battle-tested stablecoins that have maintained their pegs through multiple market cycles. USDC offers more regulatory transparency — Circle publishes regular third-party audited reserve reports and operates under US financial regulations. USDT has the larger market cap and global liquidity, making it deeply integrated across the crypto ecosystem. The practical risk difference is small for most users. On Ledn, both are held in your Transaction Account and neither is lent out — so custody risk is the same for both.
Can I earn yield on USDC or USDT on Ledn? PAA #4
USDC held on Ledn can be deposited into the Ledn Growth Account to earn yield. USDT is currently held in the Transaction Account, which does not earn yield but is available for instant trading or withdrawal. To put your stablecoins to work, consider swapping USDT to USDC (at zero fees) and then depositing into the Growth Account. Check the current rates on the Ledn Savings page.
