Can you buy Bitcoin with USDC? PAA #1
Yes. On Ledn, you can swap USDC for Bitcoin (BTC) instantly with zero trading fees. Simply deposit USDC into your Ledn Transaction Account, navigate to Trade, select the USDC/BTC direction, enter your amount, and confirm the live-quoted rate. Your BTC arrives in your account immediately. No minimum trade amount applies beyond the platform minimum.
How much is $1,000 USDC worth in Bitcoin? PAA #2
At the current rate of approximately $90,891 per BTC, $1,000 USDC is worth approximately 0.01100 BTC. This rate updates in real time on the Ledn platform — the rate shown when you confirm your trade is exactly what you receive, with no fees or spread markup added.
Is USDC the same as USD? PAA #3
USDC is a stablecoin pegged 1:1 to the US Dollar, issued by Circle (a US-regulated financial institution). Unlike USD held in a bank, USDC exists on blockchain rails — meaning you can transfer it 24/7 without banking hours or intermediaries. Each USDC is backed by US dollar reserves and short-term US Treasuries, audited regularly. On Ledn, USDC trades at approximately $1.00 USD and can be swapped for Bitcoin or other assets at any time.
What is the difference between USDC and USDT? PAA #4
Both USDC and USDT are USD-pegged stablecoins, but they differ in issuer and regulatory approach. USDC is issued by Circle, a US-regulated company that publishes regular third-party audited reserve reports — providing high transparency. USDT (Tether) is issued by Tether Ltd. and holds the largest stablecoin market cap globally, offering deep liquidity across all major exchanges. Both are widely trusted and battle-tested. Ledn supports both USDC and USDT — as well as USAT — so you can hold and trade whichever suits your needs.
