How much is $1,000 USDT worth in Bitcoin? PAA #2
At the current rate of approximately $90,891 per BTC, $1,000 USDT is worth approximately 0.01100 BTC (11,003 satoshis). This rate updates in real time on the Ledn platform — the rate shown when you confirm your trade is exactly what you receive, with no fees or spread markup added.

On Ledn, you don't "withdraw" USDT to Bitcoin — instead you swap between them on-platform instantly. Navigate to Trade, select USDT → BTC, enter the amount of USDT you want to convert, and confirm. Your BTC is credited to your Transaction Account in seconds. You can then withdraw the BTC to any external wallet at any time.
