Common questions about swapping between USDT and USAT — including the key differences between these two Tether stablecoins.

What is the difference between USDT and USAT? comparison query
Both USDT and USAT are Tether stablecoins pegged to 1 US dollar. USDT is Tether's original global stablecoin — the largest stablecoin by market cap worldwide, widely used across DeFi, CeFi, and international markets. USAT is Tether's newer US-specific stablecoin, designed to meet US regulatory requirements. On Ledn, you can hold and swap both — and convert between them instantly with zero fees.
Is USAT safer than USDT? informational
Safety depends on regulatory alignment, not just reserve backing. Both USDT and USAT are 1:1 USD-backed by Tether. USAT is specifically designed for US regulatory compliance, which may be preferable for US users or institutions with specific compliance requirements. USDT has the longer track record and larger global liquidity. Neither is inherently "safer" — they serve different regulatory contexts. On Ledn, you can hold and switch between both.
Can I swap USDT to USAT on Ledn for free? transactional
Yes. Ledn charges zero trading fees on the USDT/USAT pair. The quoted rate is exactly what you receive — no spread markup, no withdrawal fee, no hidden charges. When you confirm a swap of 1,000 USDT, you receive 1,000.10 USAT at the live rate. Nothing deducted.
Why would I swap USDT to USAT? intent
Common reasons include: migrating to a US-regulated stablecoin for compliance with US financial regulations; diversifying across multiple stablecoin issuers while staying within the Tether ecosystem; or simply because your Ledn loan was disbursed in one stablecoin and you want to convert to the other. On Ledn, the swap is instant and free — so there's no cost to moving between them.
