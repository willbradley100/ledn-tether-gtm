Gold (XAUt) / USDT — frequently asked questions FAQ schema

Answers to common questions about converting gold tokens to USDT on Ledn — and about XAUt itself.

What does 1 XAUt represent in USDT? PAA #1
1 XAUt represents exactly 1 troy ounce of physical gold. At the current gold spot price of approximately $3,185 per troy ounce, 1 XAUt is worth approximately 3,185 USDT. This rate changes continuously as the gold spot price moves — USDT itself is stable at ~$1, so the XAUt/USDT rate is essentially a direct reading of the gold price in dollars.
Can I convert gold to USDT on Ledn? PAA #2
Yes. XAUt/USDT is one of 10 integrated trading pairs on Ledn. Simply deposit XAUt to your Ledn Transaction Account, navigate to Trade, select XAUt → USDT, enter your amount, and confirm the live-quoted rate. Your USDT lands in your Transaction Account immediately — with zero fees deducted. The whole process takes seconds.
Why would I swap XAUt for USDT? PAA #3
There are several common reasons: taking profits after a gold price appreciation, rebalancing your portfolio toward a more liquid, stable-value asset, funding a loan repayment in USDT, moving value into a form you can use for purchases or off-platform transfers, or simply reducing volatility exposure during uncertain periods. The reverse — USDT to XAUt — is useful for building a gold position from a stablecoin base, or moving idle USDT into a hard asset store of value.
Is XAUt the same as physical gold? PAA #4
XAUt represents physical gold — each token is backed 1:1 by one troy ounce of gold stored in a Loomis International vault in Switzerland. You don't hold the physical bars directly (though Tether offers redemption options), but the token's value tracks the spot gold price exactly. It trades on Ethereum and TRON, meaning it can be transferred globally, 24/7, just like any crypto asset — with the value of physical gold as the underlying.
