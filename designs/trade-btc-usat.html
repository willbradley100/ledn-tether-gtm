BTC / USAT — frequently asked questions FAQ schema

Common questions about trading Bitcoin for USAT on Ledn.

What is USAT? PAA #1
USAT is Tether's US-regulated stablecoin, pegged 1:1 to the US dollar. Unlike USDT (which is offshore), USAT is designed specifically for US compliance requirements — making it Tether's answer to growing demand for a US-regulated dollar stablecoin. On Ledn, you can hold, trade, and borrow in USAT.
How much is $1,000 worth of Bitcoin in USAT? PAA #2
At the current BTC price of ~$90,891, $1,000 in Bitcoin is approximately 0.01100 BTC. Since 1 USAT = $1 USD, that converts to approximately 1,000 USAT. On Ledn, this swap happens at the live rate with zero trading fees — so the full value transfers to USAT with nothing deducted.
What is the difference between USAT and USDT? PAA #3
Both are Tether products pegged to the US dollar. USDT is Tether's original, largest-volume stablecoin — used globally with the deepest liquidity of any stablecoin. USAT is Tether's newer US-specific stablecoin, designed to meet US regulatory standards. For US-based holders who want dollar exposure with US regulatory alignment, USAT is the choice. Both are fully supported on Ledn.
Can I borrow USAT on Ledn? PAA #4
Yes. Ledn supports USAT as a loan disbursement currency. Deposit Bitcoin as collateral, choose your loan amount, and receive your loan disbursed in USAT — or USDT or USDC, your choice. No credit check is required. Rates start at 9.99% APR.
