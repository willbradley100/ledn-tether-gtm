BTC / Gold (XAUt) — frequently asked questions FAQ schema

Answers to common questions about trading Bitcoin for gold on Ledn — and about XAUt itself.

What is XAUt? PAA #1
XAUt (Tether Gold) is a digital token where each unit represents exactly one troy ounce of physical gold held in a Swiss vault. On Ledn we refer to it simply as "gold." It trades on the Ethereum and TRON blockchains and the physical gold backing can be redeemed through Tether directly. At Ledn, XAUt is one of five supported assets — you can hold it, trade it, and (coming soon) use it as collateral for loans.
How much Bitcoin can I get for 1 XAUt? PAA #2 · bitcoin gold ratio
At current prices (BTC ~$90,891, gold ~$3,185/oz), 1 XAUt is worth approximately 0.03508 BTC. This ratio — sometimes called the Bitcoin/gold ratio — changes as both asset prices move independently. Bitcoin has historically outperformed gold over 4-year cycles, but gold has held value far longer. On Ledn, you can rebalance between them instantly at any ratio, with zero fees.
Is it better to hold Bitcoin or gold? PAA #3 · bitcoin vs gold
They serve different risk profiles. Bitcoin offers potentially higher appreciation with more short-term volatility — it has outperformed gold dramatically over the past decade. Gold has millennia of track record as a store of value, a hedge against currency debasement, and is far less volatile day-to-day. Many serious wealth holders hold both. The point of the BTC/Gold (XAUt) trading pair on Ledn is that you don't have to choose permanently — rebalance between them instantly as conditions change, with no trading fees.
Where is the gold backing XAUt stored? PAA #4
Physical gold backing XAUt is stored in Loomis International vaults in Switzerland, operated by Tether. Each XAUt is verifiable on-chain, and the gold holdings are regularly attested by independent third parties. The gold is held as allocated, unencumbered bullion — meaning it is not lent out or rehypothecated. This is the same gold-custody standard used by institutional vaulting services.
