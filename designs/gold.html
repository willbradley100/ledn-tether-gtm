What is XAUt?

Gold, the way it was always meant to be held.

XAUt (Tether Gold) is a digital token where each unit represents one fine troy ounce of physical gold on a specific gold bar held in a professional vault facility in Switzerland.

Unlike paper gold or ETFs, XAUt gives you direct ownership of allocated gold that can be verified on-chain at any time — combining the timeless security of physical gold with the flexibility of digital assets.

On Ledn, we call it "gold." You'll see it referred to as Gold (XAUt) throughout our platform. Not "digital gold" or "Tether Gold" — just gold.
