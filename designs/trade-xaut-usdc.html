Gold (XAUt) / USDC — frequently asked questions FAQ schema

Common questions about trading gold (XAUt) for USDC on Ledn.

What does 1 XAUt represent in USDC? PAA #1
1 XAUt represents 1 troy ounce of physical gold held in Swiss vaults. At current gold prices, 1 XAUt is worth approximately 3,185 USDC. Since USDC is pegged to $1 USD, the XAUt/USDC rate equals the live gold spot price in US dollars per troy ounce. This rate updates in real time on the Ledn platform.
Why trade gold for USDC instead of USDT? PAA #2
Both deliver the same economic result — a dollar-pegged stablecoin with 1:1 USD value. The difference is issuer and regulatory structure. USDC is issued by Circle, a US-regulated company with monthly audited reserve reports — preferred by users who prioritise transparency. USDT (Tether) has the larger global liquidity and longer track record. Ledn supports both — choose based on your preference. Zero fees either way.
Can I earn yield on USDC on Ledn? PAA #3
Yes. USDC is available in the Ledn Growth Account to earn yield. After swapping XAUt to USDC in your Transaction Account, you can move funds to the Growth Account to start earning. Rate details are available in the Ledn app.
How is gold (XAUt) priced against USDC? PAA #4
XAUt tracks the spot gold price in USD. Since USDC is pegged to 1 US dollar, the XAUt/USDC exchange rate equals the gold spot price per troy ounce in USD. When gold trades at $3,185/oz, 1 XAUt = 3,185 USDC. As gold prices move, the XAUt/USDC rate moves with them in real time.
