# Ledn × Tether GTM — Marketing Site Updates PRD
**Status:** Draft for review  
**Go-live:** June 1, 2026 · **Site deadline:** May 25, 2026  
**Owner:** Precision Growth  
**Scope:** ledn.io marketing site only (not platform/app)

---

## Context

Ledn is launching three new Tether assets on June 1: **XAUt** (physical gold token, 1 troy oz per token, Swiss vault storage), **USDT**, and **USAT**. The platform now supports 5 assets in total (BTC, XAUt, USDT, USAT, USDC) and 10 integrated trading pairs — all with zero fees.

The site currently positions Ledn as a Bitcoin-only lending platform. These updates expand the narrative to a multi-asset wealth platform while preserving the Bitcoin-first trust equity.

**Brand rules for copy:**
- Refer to XAUt as "gold" or "XAUt" — never "digital gold" or "Tether Gold"
- Stablecoin order is always: USDT, USAT, USDC
- Platform tagline: *"Bitcoin. Gold. The stablecoins the world runs on."*

## Page Updates

### 1. Homepage — HIGH PRIORITY
**What's changing:**
- New announcement bar (XAUt gold colour) above nav
- New "platform vision" section: 3-pillar grid (Hold / Trade / Borrow) showcasing all 5 assets
- Updated loan calculator: "Funded in" selector adds USDT and USAT options

**Why:** The homepage currently has no mention of gold, USDT, or USAT. This is the highest-traffic entry point and sets the narrative for the whole launch.
